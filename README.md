## **What PikPak Actually Is (Quick Version)**

## **How to Install PikPak on Android TV: Two Methods**

Installation depends on what your TV setup looks like. There are two official methods.

### **Method 1 — Google Play Store (Google TV / Android TV with Play Store)**

If your TV or TV box runs Google TV or standard Android TV and has the Play Store available, this takes about two minutes:

1. Open the **Play Store** on your TV
2. Search for **PikPak**
3. Select the PikPak app and tap **Install**
4. Sign in with your existing account (or create one)

The Play Store handles future updates automatically. This is the cleanest install path if your TV box supports it — the Chromecast with Google TV, NVIDIA Shield, Xiaomi Mi Box, and most Sony / TCL Android TVs fall into this category.

### **Method 2 — USB Flash Drive Sideload (TV Boxes Without Play Store)**

Budget Android TV boxes — H96, X96, MX Player boxes, and most generic Chinese boxes — typically don't have Google Play Store access. For these, sideloading via USB flash drive is the standard approach:

1. On your computer, visit PikPak's official TV download page and download the **PikPak TV APK** (`PikPak_tv.apk`)
2. Copy the APK file to a USB flash drive
3. Insert the flash drive into your TV box
4. Open your TV box's **file manager app** (most boxes come with one pre-installed)
5. Locate the APK on the flash drive and tap it
6. If prompted, enable **"Install from unknown sources"** in settings — this is a standard Android security step for any sideloaded app
7. Complete the installation, then sign in to your PikPak account
